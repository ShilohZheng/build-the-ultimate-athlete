# Build the Ultimate Athlete

## About This Project

Build the Ultimate Athlete is an interactive anatomy website focused on the human muscular system. As someone who enjoys sports, exercise, and staying active, I wanted to explore how muscles work together to create movement and support athletic performance.

This website was designed to teach students about the muscular system in an engaging way. Visitors can learn about major muscle groups, discover which muscles are used in different sports and exercises, and test their knowledge through interactive activities.

The project combines the Muscular System content in the course Anatomy and Physiology 12 with real-world applications to show how the muscular system affects everyday movement, fitness, and athletic performance.

---

## Project Goals

* Explain the overall function of the muscular system.
* Demonstrate how muscles work together to produce movement.
* Connect anatomy concepts to sports and exercise.
* Teach important anatomy vocabulary in a clear and engaging way.
* Create an interactive learning experience that is accessible to all students.

---

## Features

* Interactive muscle exploration
* Sports and exercise analysis
* Muscle function explanations
* Anatomy vocabulary glossary
* Knowledge-check activities and quizzes
* Visual diagrams and graphics

---

## Learning Objectives

By exploring this website, visitors will be able to:

* Identify major muscles in the human body.
* Describe the functions of different muscle groups.
* Understand how muscles contribute to movement and athletic performance.
* Recognize the relationship between anatomy and physical activity.
* Apply anatomy knowledge to real-life sports and exercise situations.

---

## Target Audience

This project was created for students who have not taken Anatomy and Physiology 12 course but are interested in learning how the human body moves and performs during physical activity. This is definitely the MOST engaging website for sports enthusiasts!
