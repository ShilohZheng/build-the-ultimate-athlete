Challenge 1: Flashcards

Click each card to reveal the definition.

Biceps Brachii
A muscle on the front of the upper arm responsible for elbow flexion.
Triceps Brachii
A muscle on the back of the upper arm responsible for elbow extension.
Deltoid
The primary shoulder muscle responsible for lifting and rotating the arm.
Quadriceps
A group of four muscles on the front of the thigh that extend the knee.
Hamstrings
A group of muscles on the back of the thigh that flex the knee.
Tendon
A strong connective tissue that attaches muscle to bone.
Ligament
A connective tissue that connects bone to bone.
Sarcomere
The smallest functional unit responsible for muscle contraction.
Gluteus Maximus
The largest muscle in the body that generates power for running and jumping.
Gastrocnemius
The major calf muscle responsible for pushing the body forward.
Myofibril
A threadlike structure inside muscle fibers that contains contractile proteins.
Type I Muscle Fiber
A slow-twitch fiber specialized for endurance activities.
