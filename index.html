Why This Project?

Sports and exercise have always been an important part of my life. Whether playing sports, working out at the gym, or simply staying active, every movement depends on the muscular system. This project explores how muscles work together to create movement and support athletic performance.

What Is the Muscular System?

The muscular system is responsible for movement, posture, stability, and heat production. More than 600 muscles work together to help us perform everyday activities and athletic movements.

Movement

Allows the body to walk, run, jump, and perform physical activities.

Posture

Helps maintain body position while standing and sitting.

Stability

Supports joints and helps keep the body balanced.

Heat Production

Generates heat that helps regulate body temperature.

Muscle Facts

  • The human body contains over 600 muscles.
  • Skeletal muscles make up about 40% of body weight.
  • The gluteus maximus is the largest muscle in the body.
  • The masseter is one of the strongest muscles relative to its size.
